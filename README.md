<section>
    <h2>Project Overview</h2>
    <p>
        This project presents a comprehensive data analysis of district-wise IPC crimes in India
        from 2017 onwards. Using an official multi-year dataset, the study explores offence-wise
        crime patterns across districts and states, highlighting temporal trends, geographic
        hotspots, and variations across crime categories.
    </p>
</section>

<section>
    <h2>Dataset Description</h2>
    <p>
        <strong>File Source:</strong>
        <code>districtwise-ipc-crimes-2017-onwards.xlsx</code>
    </p>
    <p>
        The dataset contains detailed offence-wise crime counts at the district level,
        covering multiple years and enabling both spatial and temporal analysis.
    </p>
</section>

<section>
    <h2>Objectives / Problem Statements</h2>

    <h3>1. Temporal Trends</h3>
    <ul>
        <li>Analyze how major IPC crime categories have evolved from 2017 onwards.</li>
        <li>Study trends at national, state, and district levels.</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>2. Geographic Hotspots</h3>
    <ul>
        <li>Identify districts and states with consistently high crime volumes.</li>
        <li>Examine hotspots for specific offences such as murder, rape, and theft.</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>3. Crimes Against Women</h3>
    <ul>
        <li>Analyze the distribution of offences like rape, cruelty by husband or relatives, and sexual harassment.</li>
        <li>Study district-wise and year-wise variations.</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>4. Property and Economic Crimes</h3>
    <ul>
        <li>Examine spatial and temporal patterns of theft, robbery, and dacoity.</li>
        <li>Analyze trends in economic offences such as bank and ATM fraud.</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>5. District Crime Profiles</h3>
    <ul>
        <li>Cluster districts based on crime composition.</li>
        <li>Identify profiles such as high-violent crime districts, high-property crime districts, and mixed-pattern districts.</li>
    </ul>
</section>
